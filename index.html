This project focuses on developing a deep learning model using CNN in PyTorch to detect brain tumors from MRI images. The model automates the detection process, reducing the need for manual interpretation and enhancing accuracy. By training on labeled datasets, it improves diagnostic efficiency and supports healthcare professionals in identifying brain tumors with high precision.

This project aims to address customer churn in the competitive banking landscape by leveraging predictive analytics. Our goal is to develop models that forecast customers leaving the bank and implement targeted retention strategies to safeguard revenue streams and enhance long-term profitability.
